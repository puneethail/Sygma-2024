ABOUT

SYGMA 2024-UTOPIA

SYGMA 2024 is a prestigious National IT Fest orchestrated by the BCA Department of Shri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwara College of Business Management, Mangaluru. Our institution is dedicated to elevating the learning journey for students and nurturing their preparedness for future careers. This year, we proudly present the theme for SYGMA 2024 as "UTOPIA" – a convergence of computer science, astronomy, and space engineering, envisioning a perfect blend of technology and cosmic exploration.

Astro-informatics takes center stage, seamlessly applying computational techniques to decipher the vast complexities of astronomical data. UTOPIA invites participants to imagine a future where these disciplines harmonize, bringing forth innovative solutions and advancing our world to new heights.
